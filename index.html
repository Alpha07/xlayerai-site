🧠 What Exactly Is XLayer AI?

XLayer AI is a revolutionary platform that combines artificial intelligence, real-world hacker knowledge, and cybersecurity operations into one self-learning system. At the center of it all is our proprietary LLM — the X Intelligence Core (XIC).

It acts as a hacker when you need payloads. As a teacher when you’re trying to learn. As a malware analyst when you’re under attack. And as a strategist when you’re planning red/blue exercises. This isn’t just an AI assistant — it’s a full-blown cybersecurity brain built for the future.

🔐 SafeVault + Sandbox AI = Proactive Ransomware Defense

SafeVault watches your file system like a hawk. It catches abnormal renaming, encryption patterns, and system calls. Instead of reacting after encryption, it prevents it — by stopping the process before damage.

Sandbox AI opens up unknown files in a virtual container. It observes memory usage, registry calls, disk I/O, and flags behavior in real time — giving defenders full visibility into malware behavior, without exposing the host.
